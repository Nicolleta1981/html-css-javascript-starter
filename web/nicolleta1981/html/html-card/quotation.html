HTML Quotation and Citation Elements

  1. < abbr >Defines an abbreviation or acronym

    2. The < abbr title="World Health Organization">WHO was founded in 1948.

  2. < address >Defines contact information for the author/owner of a document
  3. < bdo >Defines the text direction and is used to override the current text direction

    4. < bdo dir="rtl">

  4. < blockquote >Defines a section that is quoted from another source

    5. Browsers usually indent blockquote elements.

    < blockquote cite="http://www.worldwildlife.org/who/index.html"> For nearly 60 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by more than one million members in the United States and close to five million globally.

  5. < cite >Defines the title of a work
  6. < q >Defines a short inline quotation
